About Laveka Guest House

Built on family values, inspired by kindness, and committed to genuine hospitality.

OUR STORY

Where the Name "Laveka" Comes From

Laveka Guest House was established by Jackson Wambua with a vision of creating a welcoming place where guests can enjoy comfort, peace, and genuine hospitality.

The name Laveka was inspired by our beloved grandmother, Rebecca Ndolo. Her kindness, generosity, and welcoming spirit continue to inspire the way we serve every guest who visits us.

Although our guest house has been serving guests for only a few years, our commitment remains the same: to provide clean, comfortable, and affordable accommodation where every visitor feels at home.

Our Foundation

Laveka Guest House is built on the belief that hospitality begins with respect, honesty, and caring for every guest as part of our extended family.

OUR FOUNDER

Jackson Wambua

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Jackson Wambua founded Laveka Guest House with a simple dream: to create a welcoming place where guests experience genuine hospitality in a peaceful and comfortable environment.

His leadership continues to guide the guest house while preserving the family values that inspired its creation.
