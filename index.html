OUR STORY

A Place Built on Family Values

Laveka Guest House was founded by Jackson Wambua with a vision of creating a welcoming place where every guest feels comfortable and valued.

The name Laveka is inspired by the memory of Rebecca Ndolo, whose kindness, generosity, and warm spirit continue to influence the way we welcome every visitor.

We believe that true hospitality is about more than providing a room—it's about creating a peaceful environment where guests feel respected, cared for, and at home.
